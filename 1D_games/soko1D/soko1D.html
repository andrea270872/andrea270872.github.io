Soko 1D

Soko1D - A mono-dimensional version of the sokoban-style games.

Main concept: the level is an acyclic graph, and the player can fold part of the graph away. Pushing and folding are the only available actions.
- To win place all the boxes in the platforms.
V0.5

Select a level:

1- Just push 2- Fold 3- Switch
4- 2 Folds 5- 2 more Folds 6- Impossible
