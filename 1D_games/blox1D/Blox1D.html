Blox 1D - A mono-dimensional version of the bloxorz-style games.
V0.5
DESCRIPTION: Move the block around. Green wedges allow you to slide the block solid, 1 step forward.
Gray dots mark emply tiles: the block will fall if you move it there. A gray tile is instead walkable.
When the block stands vertically on the yellow tile, a bridge will come up, making some of the tiles walkable.
To win place the block vertically on top of the red tile: the block must be standing on its red half!
To show that this game is indeed 1D, there is a secondary visualization (on the top-right part of this page). Try to play looking only at that "pure 1D" visualization.
- Left and Right to move
- Space slide the block, when on the special slide-tile

By Andrea Valente.
