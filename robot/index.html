Robot's memories
Instructions:
Hi, this robot is wandering around a strange world, where flowers and cakes grow spontaneously from the ground.
When the robot steps on a cake (or flower), it takes a picture and remembers it.
Each level has a goal that the robot should reach: collect pictues of flowers and cakes, so that the sequence of pictures matches matches the given, goal sequence.
Help the robot explore its world to take the pictues in the correct order and reach its goal!
