Stick&Click Game Examples

To download an play (or edit) a Stick&Click game, you need 2 files:
- a stickers file, usually called something_atlas.png
- and a rules file, usually called something_data.json

-- Games --

  • Boy and key: help the boy to open the door (with the key!)
    Stickers file: boykey_atlas.png
    Rules file: boykey_data.json
  • CastleGame: fantasy game with a warrior and a draw-bridge.
    Stickers file: castleGame_atlas.png
    Rules file: castleGame_data.json
  • Mix: not an actual game, more like a digital toy. Mix and match your monster or robot.
    Stickers file: mix_atlas.png
    Rules file: mix_data.json
  • Animals: an interactive book about animals. You can learn about the voices of animals.
    Stickers file: animals_atlas.png
    Rules file: animals_data.json
  • Combination: a game where you have to answer a question to find the right "combination" and win.
    Stickers file: combination_atlas.png
    Rules file: combination_data.json

With each game:

(1) download the 2 files, then (2) open the [player] and (3) load the 2 files to play.
